Nepali Momos

Description

Momos are popular nepali delicacy filled with meat or vegetable wrapped with flour wrappers.
It can be steamed or pan fried.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 and half pounds ground meat
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 14 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 4 tablespoons minced peeled ginger
  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with 1 and half cups of water.knead the dought until it has medium stretchability.Cover the dough for an hour.
  2. Mix the meat with all the ingredients and 2 tablespoons of salt and 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  3. To create the momos create small balls of dough.Place the ball on a flat surface and roll it into a 4-inch round with a rolling pin.
  4. Fill in about half a spoon or a spoon of your fillings on the wrapper and wrap it.
  5. Repeat the process for remaining dough and fillings.
  6. Place your momos on the steaming pan and steam it for about 8-10 minutes.
  7. To check if the momos are cooked your can touch the outer layer of momos and check if its is sticky.
    If is is not sticky then is is ready to be eaten.
