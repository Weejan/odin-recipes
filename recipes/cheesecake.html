Cheese Cake

Description

A sweet fluffy delicacy made with fresh cheese having base made with crushed cookies.
Cheese Cake may be sometime baked or unbaked and is usually is refrigerated.
Perfect to cure your sweet cravings!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla wafer cookies
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 3 quarter cup white sugar
  • 2 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat one to 175 degrees C.
  2. In a bowl mix all the Ingredients except the cookies together until you get a smooth texture.
  3. Line muffin pans with cupcake paper.
  4. Place crushed cookies in the bottom of each cupcake paper and pour the batter into the cups.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, until golden and set.
