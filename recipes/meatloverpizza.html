Meat Lover Pizza

Description

Pizza topped off with every type meat you could imagine with creamy stringy cheese and perfect curst.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon of granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 tablespoons of olive oil(optional)
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cup of mozzarella
  • 3 tablespoons of parmesan cheese
  • 3-4 slice of bacon chopped
  • 1/2 cup of ham roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup sausages chopped
  • 1/4 cup of pepperoni slices
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare dough by combining flour with warm water headed to 115 degrees F, with yeast and salt. Add salt and olive oil to the dough and knead it until the texture is smooth and let it rest for about an hour.
  2. Meanwhile, chop up the ingredients such as bacon, sausages etc.
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  4. Grease a baking sheet with olive oil.Shape the dough to desired size.
  5. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough.
  6. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella. Add the topping and finally sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or desired crispness is reached.
